## Key Metrics

* Total Revenue – Total income generated from hotel bookings.
* Total Bookings – Total number of bookings made.
* Occupancy Rate (%) – Percentage of rooms occupied during a specific period.
* Cancellation Rate (%) – Percentage of bookings cancelled.
* Utilized Capacity – Percentage of total room capacity used.

## Business Insights

* The overall occupancy rate shows that hotels are utilizing a large portion of their room capacity, indicating strong demand.
* A noticeable percentage of bookings are cancelled, which impacts revenue and occupancy performance.
* High booking numbers indicate strong customer interest, but cancellations reduce effective occupancy.
* Revenue performance is directly influenced by occupancy rate and utilized capacity.
* Improving cancellation management could significantly increase total revenue and hotel utilization.
